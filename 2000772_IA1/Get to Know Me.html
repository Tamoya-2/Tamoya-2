My Personality

If placed on a scale my likes would outweigh my dislikes 9:1. This displays my easygoing, unbothered nature. I do not dwell on whatever it is that would dampen my spirits and cause a shift in my emotions. I often indulge in my hobbies and my likes, this leaves no room for stress and normally gives me strong emotional stability. A majority of my hobbies and likes include activities which are self-suffiecient, this does not mean I reject the presence of others, in fact I welcome it, what it means is I like quiet time. I tend to not want to be a burden to other's and their space.
